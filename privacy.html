Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Nightbill ("we", "our", "the app") is a bill-splitting app for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Nightbill, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and a unique user ID provided through Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In. We do not create our own passwords or credentials.
  • Session data: Session names, items added, prices, split calculations, and participant names you enter during a session.
  • Subscription data: Whether you hold an active subscription, managed entirely through Apple's StoreKit. We do not see or store your payment method or billing details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core bill-splitting functionality (creating sessions, calculating splits, showing summaries).
  • To display your profile statistics (total spent, sessions attended, etc.).
  • To verify your subscription status so you can host sessions.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only visible to:

  • Other participants in sessions you join (they see your display name and split amounts).
  • Firebase/Google Cloud as our infrastructure provider, governed by Google's data processing terms.

5. Tracking and Analytics

Nightbill does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

6. Data Retention

Your account data and session history are retained as long as your account exists. Closed sessions remain in your history for your profile statistics.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. When you delete your account, we permanently remove your user record and authentication data from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

Nightbill is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
